**Prototype Feedback**

After creating the prototype for the Stress & Study Balance Tracker, the feedback from the users, comprising a number of students, was collected to assess the usability and usefulness of the system. The features included in the prototype are study hours tracking, stress level tracking, relaxation tips, reminder notifications, and emergency hotline resources.


Generally, the users found the system user-friendly and simple to use. The study hours tracking feature is found to be useful as the students can use this feature to monitor the study hours. The stress level tracking feature is also found to be useful as the students can use this feature to assess their mental state and be aware when their stress levels are too high.


The relaxation tips and break reminders feature is also found to be useful as the students are encouraged to take breaks during their study sessions. The emergency hotline support feature is also found to be useful as this feature is important and can be used in emergency situations when the students feel that they are under too much stress.


A number of suggestions were also made to improve the system, and these are as follows: the dashboard layout should be made simple, the emergency support feature should be made more visible, and the stress level input interface should be made simple.
